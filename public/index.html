7-stage process

Pre-filtration

This filtration separates sand, silt and residual particles from water. It also cleans muddy water and makes it fit for further processing.

Sediment filtration

Sediment filtration removes all remaining solid particles from water. This protects you and your loved ones from unwanted diseases.

Carbon filtration

This removes chlorine from water and safeguards you from health disorders due to chlorine. It also removes dissolved substances from water and impacts the taste and smell of water.

Reverse Osmosis Filtration

This filtration removes the finest of the impurities and does not allow any impurity bigger than the size of a molecule of water to pass. It forces the water through a specially designed, extremely fine membrane that stops impurities like harmful chemicals, pesticides, viruses, and more.

UV filtration

Ultra-violet filtration is the most effective filtration process in removing harmful bacteria from water. This protects you and your loved ones from various harmful diseases.

UF filtration

This is the final stage of filtration and filters any impurities that might have left unfiltered in any of the above processes. This makes the water ultra-pure.

Alkaline addition

After all the filtration, this step makes the water alkaline. An alkaline water not only helps in maintaining the pH level of the body, it also helps in preventing acid reflux and acidity in stomach. Water coming out from this stage is ultra-pure with added benefits for a healthy body.

Benefits of alkaline water

Prevents acid reflux/acidity in stomach

Helps in regulating blood pressure

Helps in case of diabetes and high cholesterol

Increases oxygen delivery in the body

Boosts immunity and keeps the body healthy

Reduces ageing and helps in weight loss
